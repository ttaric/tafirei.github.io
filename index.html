A project still in its production, the system facilitates the interaction of patients and health practitioners in Zimbabwe as a whole. The main idea is to eliminate the use of paper based health records. Electronic records which can be accessible from anywhere are the way to a better improved health sector nowadays. Patients and health practitioners can login to the system. Verified health practitioners can view health records of patients they attend to through the portal. Patients can also see their health records through their portals. Patients can ask for assistance through the portal, search for nearest doctors, hospitals or any medical centre through the portal.
